🎵 Mastering Chinese Tones

🎼 The Four Tones Explained

1st Tone (ˉ): High and flat - like singing a high note

2nd Tone (ˊ): Rising - like asking "what?" in English

3rd Tone (ˇ): Falling then rising - like "oh" when you realize something

4th Tone (ˋ): Sharp falling - like a firm "no!"

Neutral Tone: Light and quick - unstressed
